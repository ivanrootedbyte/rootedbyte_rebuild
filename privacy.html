1. Who We Are

RootedByte is a digital project that may include web apps, educational tools, faith-based reflection tools, digital wellness resources, printable products, and account-based features.

In this policy, “RootedByte,” “we,” “our,” or “us” refers to the owner and operator of the RootedByte website and related RootedByte apps or pages.

2. Scope of This Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to RootedByte websites and related pages, including but not limited to:

  • the RootedByte home page;
  • RootedOS;
  • Song DiveIn;
  • Socials & News, previously known as NewsVerse;
  • account, sign-in, or profile pages;
  • contact forms, waitlists, newsletters, or digital product pages;
  • future parent, kid, family, or educational experiences under the RootedByte name.

This policy does not apply to websites, services, or platforms that we do not control, even if they are linked from RootedByte.

3. Information We May Collect

The information we collect depends on how you use RootedByte. Some areas may be usable without creating an account. Other features may require sign-in or user-provided information.

Category Examples Why It May Be Collected
Account Information Name, email address, password authentication data, account ID, profile settings. To create accounts, allow sign-in, manage user access, and protect accounts.
Contact Information Name, email address, message content submitted through a contact form. To respond to questions, feedback, support requests, or privacy requests.
User-Generated Content Reflections, typed prompts, truth-check notes, saved app entries, preferences, or responses you choose to submit. To provide app functionality, save your progress, or personalize your experience if those features are enabled.
Usage Information Pages visited, features used, buttons clicked, general session activity, referring pages. To understand performance, improve design, fix bugs, and maintain security.
Device and Technical Information Browser type, device type, operating system, approximate location from IP address, error logs. To keep the website working properly, troubleshoot issues, and protect against abuse.
Payment Information Purchase status, order details, product purchased, payment confirmation. Full payment card details are usually handled by third-party payment providers. To process purchases, deliver digital products, provide receipts, and handle support requests.

Information You Provide Directly

You may provide information when you create an account, sign in, submit a form, request support, download a product, join a waitlist, or use interactive features.

Information Collected Automatically

Like many websites, RootedByte or its service providers may automatically receive limited technical information when you visit the site. This can include browser, device, IP address, timestamps, referring pages, and basic usage logs.

Information We Do Not Intentionally Collect

RootedByte is not designed to collect highly sensitive personal information such as government identification numbers, financial account numbers, medical records, or precise location data. Please do not submit sensitive information into RootedByte forms or app fields unless a feature clearly asks for it and explains why.

4. How We Use Information

We may use collected information for the following purposes:

  • to operate, maintain, and improve RootedByte;
  • to create, authenticate, and manage user accounts;
  • to save user preferences or app content when account-based features are enabled;
  • to respond to messages, questions, or support requests;
  • to deliver digital products, downloads, or email confirmations;
  • to troubleshoot technical issues, fix bugs, and improve performance;
  • to understand how users interact with RootedByte features;
  • to protect the website from spam, misuse, fraud, or unauthorized access;
  • to comply with applicable legal, security, or regulatory obligations.

We do not use personal information for purposes that are materially different from those described in this policy unless we update this policy or provide additional notice where appropriate.

5. Accounts and Authentication

If RootedByte offers account creation or sign-in, account information may be processed by a third-party authentication or database provider, such as Supabase or a similar service.

Account-related information may include your email address, encrypted password authentication data, account ID, login timestamps, and security-related metadata. Passwords should not be stored by RootedByte in plain readable text.

You are responsible for keeping your account login information secure. Please use a strong password and do not share your account credentials with others.

Account Deletion

If account deletion is available inside the website, you may use that feature to request removal of your account. If no self-serve deletion tool is available yet, you may contact RootedByte to request account deletion.

Some limited information may need to be retained for security, legal, backup, abuse-prevention, or transaction record purposes.

6. Children and Family Use

RootedByte may include content designed for families, parents, or children. Parents and guardians should supervise children’s use of RootedByte, especially when a feature allows typed input, account creation, or saved activity.

RootedByte does not knowingly seek to collect personal information from children without appropriate parental involvement or consent where required by law. If we learn that a child has provided personal information without appropriate consent, we will take reasonable steps to delete or limit that information.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has submitted personal information through RootedByte, please contact us so we can review and respond.

For Parent/Kid Features

If RootedByte later launches a dedicated parent/kid experience, we may add more specific child privacy details, including what children can submit, whether parents can review or delete information, and whether any data is shared with third-party providers.

7. Third-Party Services

RootedByte may rely on trusted third-party services to operate the website and provide features. These providers may process limited information as needed to perform services for RootedByte.

Examples may include:

  • Hosting providers, such as Vercel or similar platforms;
  • Authentication and database providers, such as Supabase or similar services;
  • Payment providers, such as PayPal, Stripe, or similar services;
  • Email or form providers, for contact messages, newsletters, or waitlists;
  • Analytics providers, if analytics are enabled;
  • Cloud storage providers, for downloadable files or digital products.

These third-party services have their own privacy and security practices. RootedByte is not responsible for the privacy practices of external websites or platforms that we do not control.

8. Cookies, Analytics, and Local Storage

RootedByte may use cookies, browser storage, or similar technologies to keep the website functional, remember preferences, improve performance, or understand how users interact with the site.

Essential Storage

Some storage may be necessary for sign-in sessions, security, navigation, or saving basic preferences.

Analytics

If analytics are used, they may help us understand general usage patterns, such as which pages are visited, what features are used, and where technical errors occur. We should aim to use privacy-conscious analytics wherever practical.

Your Browser Choices

Most browsers allow you to block, delete, or control cookies and local storage. Some RootedByte features may not work correctly if essential cookies or storage are disabled.

9. AI and Reflection Features

Some RootedByte tools may include reflection prompts, truth-check features, song or media reflection tools, or other AI-assisted experiences.

If an AI-assisted feature is used, text you submit may be processed to generate a response or reflection. Please avoid entering private, sensitive, or confidential information into AI or reflection fields.

AI-generated responses may be imperfect and should not be treated as professional advice, legal advice, medical advice, financial advice, or pastoral authority. Users should exercise personal judgment and, where appropriate, seek help from trusted qualified people.

10. Payments and Digital Products

RootedByte may offer printable resources, digital downloads, subscriptions, donations, or other products. Payment processing may be handled by third-party payment providers.

RootedByte generally should not directly receive or store full credit card numbers. Payment providers may collect and process payment details according to their own privacy policies and security standards.

We may receive limited transaction information, such as your name, email address, product purchased, order ID, payment status, and delivery information, so we can provide access, receipts, refunds, or customer support.

11. When Information May Be Shared

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information only when reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this policy.

Information may be shared with:

  • service providers that help operate RootedByte;
  • payment processors when you make a purchase;
  • hosting, database, and authentication providers;
  • support or communication tools used to respond to you;
  • legal, safety, or security authorities if required by law or necessary to protect rights, safety, or security;
  • a successor organization if RootedByte is ever transferred, merged, or reorganized.

We do not intentionally share personal user content publicly unless you choose to make it public or a feature clearly explains that your submission will be visible to others.

12. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect personal information from unauthorized access, misuse, loss, or disclosure. These steps may include account authentication, provider-level security protections, access controls, encryption where supported, and limiting access to information where practical.

However, no website, database, or online service can guarantee perfect security. You use RootedByte with the understanding that internet-based services carry some level of risk.

If we become aware of a data incident that affects personal information, we will take reasonable steps to investigate and respond according to applicable obligations.

13. Data Retention

We keep personal information only as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

Retention periods may depend on:

  • whether you have an active account;
  • whether the information is needed to provide a service or product;
  • whether the information is needed for security, fraud prevention, or abuse prevention;
  • whether the information relates to payments, tax, accounting, or legal records;
  • whether backups or logs temporarily retain information after deletion.

When information is no longer needed, we will take reasonable steps to delete, anonymize, or limit access to it.

14. Your Privacy Choices and Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights regarding your personal information. These may include the right to:

  • request access to personal information we hold about you;
  • request correction of inaccurate information;
  • request deletion of your account or certain personal information;
  • withdraw consent where processing is based on consent;
  • object to or restrict certain processing;
  • request a copy of certain information in a portable format;
  • make a complaint to a privacy regulator where applicable.

To make a privacy request, contact us using the contact details below. We may need to verify your identity before completing certain requests.

15. International Users

If you access RootedByte from outside the country where it is operated, you understand that your information may be processed in jurisdictions with different data protection laws.

When this policy is updated, the “Last updated” date at the top of the page will be changed. Significant changes may also be communicated through the website or account-related notices where appropriate.
