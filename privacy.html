1. Who We Are

RootedByte is a digital project that may include web apps, educational tools, truth-rooted reflection tools, digital wellness resources, printable products, and account-based features.

In this policy, “RootedByte,” “we,” “our,” or “us” refers to the owner and operator of the RootedByte website and related RootedByte apps or pages.

2. Scope of This Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to RootedByte websites and related pages, including but not limited to:

  • the RootedByte home page;
  • Inner Work, previously known as RootedOS;
  • SoundSense, previously known as Song DiveIn;
  • Signal, previously known as Socials & News or NewsVerse;
  • account, sign-in, or profile pages;
  • contact forms, waitlists, newsletters, or digital product pages;
  • future parent, kid, family, or educational experiences under the RootedByte name.

This policy does not apply to websites, services, or platforms that we do not control, even if they are linked from RootedByte.

3. Information We May Collect

The information we collect depends on how you use RootedByte. Some areas may be usable without creating an account. Other features may require sign-in or user-provided information.

Category Examples Why It May Be Collected
Account Information Name, email address, password authentication data, account ID, profile settings. To create accounts, allow sign-in, manage user access, and protect accounts.
Contact Information Name, email address, message content submitted through a contact form. To respond to questions, feedback, support requests, or privacy requests.
User-Generated Content Reflections, typed prompts, truth-check notes, saved app entries, preferences, or responses you choose to submit. To provide app functionality, save your progress, or personalize your experience if those features are enabled.
Usage Information Pages visited, features used, buttons clicked, general session activity, referring pages. To understand performance, improve design, fix bugs, and maintain security.
Device and Technical Information Browser type, device type, operating system, approximate location from IP address, error logs. To keep the website working properly, troubleshoot issues, and protect against abuse.
Payment Information Purchase status, order details, product purchased, payment confirmation. Full payment card details are usually handled by third-party payment providers. To process purchases, deliver digital products, provide receipts, and handle support requests.

Information You Provide Directly

You may provide information when you create an account, sign in, submit a form, request support, download a product, join a waitlist, or use interactive features.

Information Collected Automatically

Like many websites, RootedByte or its service providers may automatically receive limited technical information when you visit the site. This can include browser, device, IP address, timestamps, referring pages, and basic usage logs.

Information We Do Not Intentionally Collect

RootedByte is not designed to collect highly sensitive personal information such as government identification numbers, financial account numbers, medical records, or precise location data. Please do not submit sensitive information into RootedByte forms or app fields unless a feature clearly asks for it and explains why.

We may use collected information for the following purposes:

  • to operate, maintain, and improve RootedByte;
  • to create, authenticate, and manage user accounts;
  • to save user preferences or app content when account-based features are enabled;
  • to respond to messages, questions, or support requests;
  • to deliver digital products, downloads, or email confirmations;
  • to troubleshoot technical issues, fix bugs, and improve performance;
  • to understand how users interact with RootedByte features;
  • to protect the website from spam, misuse, fraud, or unauthorized access;
  • to comply with applicable legal, security, or regulatory obligations.

You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your login credentials and for using a secure password.
